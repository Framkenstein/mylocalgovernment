How We Calculate the Scores

Our system shows how often council members vote FOR policies that advance Santa Cruz's goals.

The Scoring Formula

We calculate a score for each category based on voting records:

Score = Pro Votes / Total Votes Present

Example: 13 pro votes out of 15 total votes present = 13/15 = 0.87 (87% alignment)

Pro Votes

"Yes" votes on policies that advance the category goal (e.g., new housing projects for Housing category).

Against Votes

"No" votes on policies that advance the category goal, or "Yes" votes on restrictive policies.

Fairness

Only votes where members were present count. Absences don't hurt scores - they just reduce the total possible points.
